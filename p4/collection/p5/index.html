Wolf Link

Release Date: 03/04/2016
Presented by: Nintendo

Wolf Link is the legendary twilit beast form of the hero Link™. He first appeared in the Legend of Zelda™: Twilight Princess game, where he worked with the mysterious and mischievous imp Midna™ to take down a usurper to the throne, and to save Hyrule and the Twilight Realm from darkness.
