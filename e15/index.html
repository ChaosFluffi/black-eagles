Drawing Instructions

    Create a document 1000x1000 px.

    Make a square 31x31px in the document centered both vertically and horizontally on the page

    Color this square the color of your hair

    Make a square 235x235px and align it adjacent to the left of first square, and center it vertically on the page.

    Color this square #808080

    Make a rectangle 31px tall and 165px wide, and align it adjacent to the right of the original square, and center it vertically

    Color this rectangle the color of your skin

    Make a line segment from the top left corner of the large square that stretches from that corner to a position 118px to the right, and 62px up

    Make a line segment from the top right corner of the large square that stretches from that corner to a position 117px to the left, and 62px up

    Make a line segment from the bottom left corner of the large square that stretches from that corner to a position 118px to the right, and 48px down

    Make a line segment from the bottom right corner of the large square that stretches from that corner to a position 117px to the left, and 48px down
